## For Employees (5 Steps)

### Your First Day

**What You Need:**
- Employee Number (e.g., EMP001)
- 4-6 Digit PIN (provided by supervisor)
- Location of time clock kiosk

---

### 1. Get Your Employee Number and PIN

Ask your supervisor for:
- **Employee Number:** EMP001, EMP002, etc.
- **Kiosk PIN:** Your secret 4-6 digit code (like 1234)

> **Keep Your PIN Secret!** Never share it with coworkers.

---

### 2. Go to Kiosk at Your Location

**Find the time clock kiosk:**
- Usually located at the entrance of your work area
- Looks like a tablet or computer with a camera
- Screen displays "DetailHub Punch Clock"

---

### 3. Enter ID → PIN → Take Photo → Clock In

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Enter Employee Number**
   - Type your Employee Number (e.g., EMP001)

2. **Enter PIN**
   - Type your 4-6 digit PIN
   - It will show as dots (•••••) for security

3. **Click "Clock In" Button**
   - Big green button

4. **Take Your Photo**
   - Look directly at the camera
   - Remove sunglasses and hats
   - Stay within the green guide box
   - Click "Capture"

5. **Confirm Photo**
   - Review your photo
   - Click "Confirm" if it looks good
   - Or click "Retake" if you need to try again

6. **Success!**
   - You will see a green checkmark
   - Your clock-in time is displayed
   - You are now on the clock

> **[Quick Reference Card: Clock In Process]**
> Print this and laminate for kiosk wall display.

---

### 4. Take 30 Min Break for Shifts > 6 Hours

**When You Must Take a Break:**
- Required by law for shifts over 6 hours
- Usually during lunch hours (11:30 AM - 1:00 PM)

**How to Start Break:**
1. Go to kiosk
2. Enter ID and PIN
3. Click orange "Start Break" button
4. Take photo
5. Enjoy your break!

**How to End Break:**
1. Return to kiosk after 30 minutes
2. Enter ID and PIN
3. Click orange "End Break" button
4. Take photo
5. Resume work

> **Important:** Breaks are unpaid, so don't forget to clock out and back in!

---

### 5. Clock Out at End of Shift

**How to Clock Out:**

1. Go to kiosk
2. Enter Employee Number and PIN
3. Click red "Clock Out" button
4. Take photo one last time
5. Confirm photo
6. You will see a summary of today's hours

**Before You Leave:**
- Make sure you see "Clocked Out Successfully"
- Check the summary shows correct hours
- If something looks wrong, tell your supervisor immediately

---

## Photo Capture Tips (Quick Reference)

### ✅ DO:
- Face camera directly
- Good lighting
- Remove sunglasses/hats
- Stay within green guide box
- Hold still for 1-2 seconds

### ❌ DON'T:
- Look away from camera
- Wear sunglasses or mask
- Cover your face with hands
- Move around while capturing
- Rush through the process

## For Supervisors (5 Steps)

### Your Daily Workflow

---

### 1. Login to DetailHub Dashboard

**Access:**
- **URL:** https://your-dealership.mydetailarea.com
- **Username:** Your email address
- **Password:** Provided during onboarding

**Dashboard Overview:**
- 8 tabs: Overview, Employees, Schedules, Timecards, Analytics, Reports, Invoices, Kiosks

---

### 2. Create Employee Schedules for the Week

**Navigate to Schedules Tab:**

1. **Click "Schedules" in the top navigation**

2. **Select the Week**
   - Use Previous/Next Week buttons to navigate

3. **Click a Time Slot**
   - Click on a day/time to create a shift
   - Example: Monday, 8:00 AM

4. **Fill in Shift Details**
   - Employee: Select from dropdown
   - Start Time: 8:00 AM
   - End Time: 5:00 PM
   - Assigned Kiosk: KIOSK-001
   - Break: 30 minutes (unpaid) if shift is 6+ hours

5. **Click "Create Shift"**
   - Shift appears on the calendar
   - Employee can now clock in during this window

**Pro Tip:** Create schedules at least 7 days in advance so employees know their shifts.

---

### 3. Review Pending Photo Verifications Daily

**Navigate to Timecards Tab:**

1. **Click "Timecards" in the top navigation**

2. **Look for Yellow Alert Box**
   - At the top: "Pending Photo Reviews: X entries"
   - Only appears when there are photos awaiting approval

3. **Review Each Photo**
   - Employee name and time
   - Photo captured from kiosk
   - Action (Clock In, Clock Out, Break Start, Break End)

4. **Approve or Reject**
   - **Approve:** Click green "Approve" button if photo is valid
   - **Reject:** Click red "Reject" button if photo is suspicious

**What to Look For:**
- ✅ Photo clearly shows employee's face
- ✅ Employee is the correct person
- ✅ Photo is not blurry or obstructed
- ❌ Wrong person (buddy punching)
- ❌ Face is covered (mask, sunglasses, hat)

---

### 4. Approve/Reject Punches

**Approval Process:**

- **Approve:** Time entry is finalized and counted toward payroll
- **Reject:** Time entry is flagged and must be manually corrected

**After Rejection:**
1. Contact the employee to investigate
2. Manually correct the timecard if needed
3. Add a note explaining the correction

---

### 5. Export Timecards on Fridays

**Before Submitting Payroll:**

1. **Navigate to Reports Tab**

2. **Select "Payroll Report"**

3. **Set Date Range**
   - Example: 01/01/2025 to 01/15/2025 (bi-weekly)

4. **Click "Generate Report"**

5. **Review for Accuracy**
   - Spot-check a few employees
   - Verify totals look reasonable
   - Check for missing hours or anomalies

6. **Export to CSV or Excel**
   - Click "Export" button
   - Choose format (CSV for most payroll systems)
   - Download file

7. **Send to Payroll/HR**
   - Email the file to your payroll processor
   - Or upload to your payroll system

**Pro Tip:** Run a draft report mid-week to catch errors early!

## For Managers (5 Steps)

### System Setup and Oversight

---

### 1. Configure Kiosks (One-Time Setup)

**Navigate to Kiosks Tab:**

1. **Click "Kiosks" in the top navigation**

2. **Click "Add Kiosk"**

3. **Fill in Kiosk Details**
   - Kiosk ID: KIOSK-001
   - Location Name: "Service Bay - East Entrance"
   - Dealership: Select your dealership
   - Status: Active

4. **Click "Create Kiosk"**

5. **Set Up Hardware**
   - Install tablet or computer with camera
   - Navigate to: `https://your-dealership.com/punch-clock?kiosk_id=KIOSK-001`
   - Bookmark the URL
   - Test camera access

**Pro Tip:** Mount kiosks near entrance/exit points where all employees pass.

---

### 2. Create Employees and Assign PINs

**Navigate to Employees Tab:**

1. **Click "Employees" in the top navigation**

2. **Click "Add Employee"**

3. **Fill in Required Information**
   - First Name: John
   - Last Name: Smith
   - Role: Detailer
   - Department: Detail
   - Hourly Rate: $25.00
   - Hire Date: Select from calendar
   - Status: Active
   - Kiosk PIN: 1234 (assign a secure PIN)

4. **Optional: Enable Auto-Generated Schedules**
   - Check "Auto-Generate Schedules"
   - Set default shift times (e.g., 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM)
   - Select work days (e.g., Mon-Fri)
   - Set default break: 30 minutes (unpaid)

5. **Click "Add Employee"**
   - Employee Number is auto-generated (EMP001)
   - Give the employee their number and PIN

**Pro Tip:** Use strong PINs (not 1234 or 0000) to prevent time theft.

---

### 3. Set Up Shift Templates

**For Employees with Recurring Shifts:**

1. **Edit Employee Record**
   - Go to Employees tab
   - Click "Edit" next to the employee

2. **Enable Auto-Generate Schedules**
   - Check the box

3. **Configure Template**
   - Shift Start: 08:00
   - Shift End: 17:00
   - Work Days: Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday
   - Break: 30 minutes (unpaid)
   - Days Ahead: 30 (generate schedules 30 days in advance)

4. **Click "Save"**

**Result:**
- System automatically generates schedules for the next 30 days
- Schedules are created nightly at midnight
- Saves supervisors time on manual scheduling

---

### 4. Review Analytics Weekly

**Navigate to Analytics Tab:**

1. **Click "Analytics" in the top navigation**

2. **Select Date Range**
   - Use quick presets: Last 7 Days, Last 30 Days, Last 90 Days
   - Or use the date picker for custom ranges

3. **Review Key Metrics**
   - **Total Employees:** How many worked during this period
   - **Total Hours:** Sum of all hours (regular + overtime)
   - **Total Overtime:** Hours over 40/week (should be <5% of total)
   - **Average Hours per Employee:** Should be consistent (within 10%)

4. **Analyze Trends**
   - **Hours by Employee:** Identify top performers and burnout risks
   - **Hours by Department:** See which departments are over/under-staffed
   - **Attendance Patterns:** Identify busy vs slow days

5. **Take Action**
   - If overtime is high, adjust schedules to distribute hours more evenly
   - If attendance is low, investigate absences
   - If department hours are imbalanced, shift resources

**Pro Tip:** Review analytics every Monday morning to plan the week ahead.

---

### 5. Generate Payroll Reports Monthly

**Navigate to Reports Tab:**

1. **Click "Reports" in the top navigation**

2. **Select Report Type: "Payroll Report"**

3. **Set Date Range**
   - Example (Bi-weekly): 01/01/2025 to 01/15/2025
   - Example (Semi-monthly): 01/01/2025 to 01/15/2025

4. **Optional: Filter by Department or Employee**
   - Leave blank to include all employees

5. **Click "Generate Report"**

6. **Review Report Preview**
   - Check for missing hours
   - Verify overtime calculations
   - Look for break violations

7. **Export to CSV/Excel/PDF**
   - Click "Export"
   - Choose format (CSV recommended for payroll systems)
   - Download file

8. **Submit to Payroll**
   - Email to payroll processor or HR
   - Or upload to your payroll system

**Pro Tip:** Run a draft report mid-period to catch errors early!

## Emergency Procedures

### Kiosk is Down
1. Record employee arrival time on paper
2. Notify IT support immediately
3. Manually enter punch times later
4. Add note: "Manual punch - kiosk was offline"

### Employee Forgot PIN
1. Go to Employees tab
2. Find the employee
3. Click "Edit"
4. Update the PIN field
5. Notify the employee of their new PIN

### Power Outage
1. Record all punch times on paper
2. Note the time the outage occurred
3. When power is restored, manually enter all punches
4. Add notes explaining the situation

## Key Concepts

### Employee Number
- Format: EMP001, EMP002, etc.
- Auto-generated when creating an employee
- Permanent (never changes)
- Used for logging in at kiosks

### Kiosk PIN
- 4-6 digit secret code (like 1234)
- Set by supervisor when creating employee
- Can be reset if forgotten
- Must be kept confidential

### Photo Verification
- Captures employee's face at clock-in/out
- Prevents buddy punching (time theft)
- Requires supervisor approval
- Stored securely for 7 years

### Schedule Window
- Employees can clock in 5 minutes before scheduled start
- Example: 8:00 AM shift → can clock in at 7:55 AM
- Prevents late flags for early arrivals

### Break Policy
- Shifts under 6 hours: No break required
- Shifts 6+ hours: 30-minute unpaid break required
- Federal law (FLSA) compliance
- System flags violations automatically

### Overtime
- Hours over 40 per week
- Paid at 1.5× normal rate (time-and-a-half)
- Example: $20/hr regular → $30/hr overtime
- Must be approved by supervisor in advance
